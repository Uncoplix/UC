Balloon.earth – Explore new cities from above.

Balloon.earth is a web experiment created by Hinderling Volkart' Lab combining real time weather data with 3D map renderings. We unveil the beauty of our planet giving you a new city to discover every tab. Tilt the view and explore the beauty of human made landscapes. We call it digital ballooning.

  • Font in use: Grilli type pressura
  • Data source: Google Maps, Open Weather Map, Nasa
